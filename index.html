Welcome to Pure Fitness

Pure Fitness is a Bristol based gym, offering a wide range of excercise equipment and free weights.

We have daily classes for all levels of fitness.

Contact us for a tour of the gym and free session.

Feel Healthy

Shape up and burn fat as you lunge, step and squat your way to fitness in our ever-popular, fun classes using both weights and your own bodyweight.

Great Excercise

Physical exercise, weightlifting included, is known to be better at burning calories than aerobic excercise, leading to more weight loss.

Get Social

Excercise with others, or attend our classes. Social excercise can pull you out of a running funk or low mood, inspire you to try new events

Feel Strong

Weightlifting strengthens your heart by pumping blood more effectively through your body.
